# SaaS Marketing Agents - Workflow Examples

This directory contains documented examples of how multiple agents collaborate on real B2B SaaS marketing campaigns. Each workflow shows the orchestration, handoff points, and deliverables across different marketing disciplines.

## Overview

The SaaS Marketing Agents framework enables complex marketing operations through coordinated multi-agent workflows. These examples demonstrate:

- **Agent Specialization**: Each agent has focused expertise in specific domains (positioning, content, paid media, email, social, analytics, etc.)
- **Sequential Handoffs**: Agents receive output from upstream agents and produce deliverables for downstream agents
- **Parallel Execution**: Many agents can work simultaneously on complementary tasks
- **Integration Points**: Clear integration points where agent outputs become inputs for other agents
- **Quality Gates**: Validation checkpoints ensure work meets standards before proceeding

## Agent Categories

The SaaS Marketing Agents framework includes agents across these disciplines:

- **Product Marketing**: Positioning, messaging, competitive intelligence, launch management
- **Content**: Blog, thought leadership, case studies, whitepapers, video scripts
- **SEO**: Keyword research, content optimization, technical SEO
- **Paid Media**: PPC, social ads, programmatic buying, budget optimization, attribution
- **Social**: LinkedIn strategy, community management, influencer outreach
- **Email**: Lifecycle architecture, copywriting, automation, deliverability
- **Design**: Visual design, landing pages, ads, brand identity
- **Sales Enablement**: Outbound strategies, sales content, competitive objection handling
- **Analytics**: Data analysis, pipeline tracking, reporting, insights
- **Project Management**: Timeline planning, workflow coordination, resource management
- **Client Operations**: Legal compliance, financial tracking, quality assurance
- **Strategy**: Market research, customer insights, strategic planning

## Common Patterns

### Discovery & Research Phase
Multiple agents often work in parallel on foundational research:
- Customer Insights Researcher gathers primary research
- Competitive Intelligence identifies competitive landscape
- Keyword Researcher identifies SEO opportunities
- These feed downstream strategic decisions

### Strategy & Planning Phase
Strategic agents synthesize research into actionable plans:
- Positioning Strategist defines market positioning
- Blog Strategist plans content calendar
- PPC Strategist develops campaign structure
- Budget Optimizer allocates resources

### Execution & Production Phase
Tactical agents execute the plans:
- Copywriter produces content and ad copy
- Ad Creative Producer creates visual assets
- Landing Page Specialist builds conversion experiences
- Email Lifecycle Architect builds nurture sequences

### Distribution & Measurement Phase
Distribution agents amplify and analytics track impact:
- PPC Strategist launches paid campaigns
- LinkedIn Strategist runs organic social
- Newsletter Curator distributes via email
- Pipeline Analyst tracks results and attribution
