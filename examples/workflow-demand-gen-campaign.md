# Demand Generation Campaign Workflow

## Overview

A comprehensive multi-agent workflow for executing a full-funnel demand generation campaign from strategy through results analysis. This workflow shows coordination across customer research, paid media, conversion optimization, and analytics over a 4-6 week execution cycle.

**Timeline**: 4-6 weeks from strategy to full optimization
**Agents**: 7-9 specialized agents
**Budget**: $10K-$100K+ (flexible based on company size)
**Expected Outcomes**: 200-1,000 MQLs, $500K-$5M+ in influenced pipeline

---

## Phase 1: Strategy & Audience Definition (Weeks 1-2)

### Customer Insights Researcher

**Activities**

- **ICP (Ideal Customer Profile) Analysis**
  - Review existing customer data and win/loss data
  - Conduct 8-12 customer interviews to understand common characteristics
  - Analyze firmographic data (company size, industry, location, funding stage)
  - Identify psychographic characteristics (attitudes, behaviors, values)
  - Document technical vs. business profiles

- **Decision-Making Process Research**
  - Map buyer journey from problem awareness to decision
  - Identify key stakeholders involved in purchase decision
  - Document each stakeholder's primary concerns and success criteria
  - Identify key buying signals and trigger events
  - Map content and messaging needs at each stage

- **Pain Point & Motivation Analysis**
  - Document top 5-7 pain points driving purchase decisions
  - Identify quantifiable business impact of these pain points
  - Map which pain points matter to different stakeholders
  - Identify false solutions customers might consider instead
  - Document proof points that validate your solution

- **Competitive Consideration Analysis**
  - Document primary competitors considered by buyers
  - Understand how buyers compare you to alternatives
  - Identify common objections and concerns about your product
  - Document what helps buyers choose you
  - Identify messaging that resonates most

**Deliverables**

- **ICP Definition Document** (5-10 pages)
  - Detailed persona profiles (titles, responsibilities, goals, concerns)
  - Firmographic criteria (company size, industry, location, etc.)
  - Behavioral characteristics
  - Buying authority and decision process
  - Estimated market size for ICP

- **Buyer Journey Map** (3-5 pages)
  - Journey from problem awareness to decision
  - Key stakeholders at each stage
  - Content and messaging needs at each stage
  - Key questions and concerns at each stage
  - Trigger events and buying signals

- **Pain Point & Motivation Analysis** (3-5 pages)
  - Top pain points ranked by priority
  - Quantified business impact
  - Stakeholder-specific concerns
  - Supporting proof points and evidence

---

### Positioning Strategist

**Activities**

- **Demand Gen Positioning Workshop**
  - Review customer insights from researcher
  - Conduct positioning alignment workshop
  - Validate positioning against discovered pain points
  - Identify messaging angles most resonant with target audience
  - Define competitive differentiation for demand gen campaign

- **Campaign-Specific Messaging Development**
  - Develop 3-5 core message pillars for campaign
  - Create compelling problem statement for target audience
  - Develop evidence of solution effectiveness
  - Create competitive differentiation messaging
  - Define CTAs and value propositions for campaign

**Deliverables**

- **Campaign Positioning Brief** (3-5 pages)
  - Problem statement and market frame
  - Positioning statement tailored to campaign audience
  - 3-5 core messaging pillars
  - Competitive differentiation
  - Proof points and evidence

---

### Budget Optimizer

**Activities**

- **Historical Campaign Analysis**
  - Review ROI of past demand gen campaigns
  - Analyze CAC (cost per acquisition) by channel
  - Document conversion rates at each stage (click → lead → MQL → SQL → customer)
  - Identify which channels drive highest quality leads
  - Calculate customer lifetime value for ROI context

- **Budget Allocation Modeling**
  - Define total campaign budget
  - Model budget allocation across channels (PPC, social, email, content, design)
  - Project expected results by channel (impressions, clicks, leads, conversions)
  - Identify optimal budget split based on historical performance
  - Build contingency for testing and optimization

- **Performance Targets Setting**
  - Define target CPL (cost per lead) by channel and campaign
  - Set target ROAS (return on ad spend) or CAC payback period
  - Define success metrics for campaign
  - Set monthly budget pacing
  - Identify breakeven points and optimization thresholds

**Deliverables**

- **Budget Allocation Plan** (2-3 pages)
  - Total campaign budget
  - Channel allocation (specific amounts and percentages)
  - Monthly pacing schedule
  - Performance targets by channel (CPL, conversion rates, ROAS)
  - Contingency budget for optimization

## Phase 2: Campaign Setup & Creative Development (Weeks 2-4)

### PPC Strategist

**Activities**

- **Campaign Architecture**
  - Define account structure and campaign organization
  - Plan keyword strategy (branded, competitive, intent-based)
  - Research 500+ keywords with search volume and competition data
  - Group keywords by intent and create ad group structure
  - Plan landing page assignments for different keyword clusters

- **Ad Strategy & Creative Brief**
  - Define messaging angles for different keyword groups
  - Create ad copy variations for testing (5-10 variations)
  - Define landing page requirements for conversion optimization
  - Plan callout extensions, sitelinks, and other ad extensions
  - Define conversion tracking and attribution strategy

- **Bidding & Optimization Strategy**
  - Choose bidding strategy (CPC vs. ECPC vs. target CPA)
  - Set initial bid levels based on target CPL and conversion rates
  - Plan bid adjustment strategy by device, location, time
  - Define daily budget and pacing
  - Plan continuous optimization approach

**Deliverables**

- **PPC Campaign Plan** (10-15 pages)
  - Account and campaign structure document
  - Master keyword list (500+ keywords with metrics)
  - Keyword grouping and ad group mapping
  - Ad copy variations for each ad group
  - Landing page assignment matrix
  - Conversion tracking specifications
  - Bidding and optimization strategy

---

### Social Ads Specialist

**Activities**

- **Audience & Targeting Strategy**
  - Define target audience segments (job title, company size, industry, interests)
  - Research LinkedIn, Facebook, and Instagram targeting options
  - Plan account-based marketing (ABM) targeting for key accounts
  - Define lookalike audiences based on customer lists
  - Plan retargeting audiences from website visitors and leads

- **Campaign Structure & Creative Briefs**
  - Define campaign objectives (lead generation, conversions, etc.)
  - Plan 3-4 distinct campaign angles for testing
  - Create creative briefs for each angle
  - Define messaging and offers for social ads
  - Plan landing page and form strategy

- **Platform-Specific Strategy**
  - LinkedIn strategy (job title targeting, company targeting, account-based)
  - Facebook/Instagram strategy (interest targeting, lookalike, DPA)
  - Retargeting strategy across all platforms
  - Lead form strategy for native platform lead capture
  - Platform-specific creative specifications

**Deliverables**

- **Social Ads Campaign Plan** (8-12 pages)
  - Campaign structure and naming convention
  - Detailed audience definitions for each campaign
  - Audience sizing estimates
  - Creative briefs and messaging angles
  - Landing page and form specifications
  - Performance targets and optimization approach

---

### Landing Page Specialist

**Activities**

- **Landing Page Architecture**
  - Review PPC and social campaign requirements
  - Plan 2-4 distinct landing page variations for different audiences/offers
  - Design page layout and conversion elements
  - Plan form complexity and field strategy
  - Design thank you page and post-conversion experience

- **Conversion Optimization**
  - Define compelling value proposition and headline
  - Plan form fields (minimize for higher conversion but capture key data)
  - Design clear CTA and button copy
  - Plan page copy for different visitor types
  - Define trust elements and social proof placement

- **A/B Testing Strategy**
  - Plan primary A/B test hypothesis (headline vs. CTA vs. form fields)
  - Define test duration and sample size requirements
  - Plan secondary test hypotheses
  - Set statistical significance requirements

**Deliverables**

- **Landing Page Strategy & Designs** (detailed specs)
  - 2-4 landing page design options with wireframes
  - High-fidelity designs for approved variations
  - Page copy for each element
  - Form field strategy and CTA copy
  - A/B testing plan with success metrics
  - Mobile responsiveness specifications

---

### Ad Creative Producer

**Activities**

- **Creative Concept Development**
  - Review campaign messaging angles
  - Develop 3-5 creative concepts
  - Design visual approaches for ads
  - Create ad copy and headline variations
  - Plan asset specifications by platform

- **Creative Production**
  - Design 8-12 ad variations for testing
  - Create LinkedIn ad formats (image, video, carousel, document)
  - Create Facebook/Instagram ads (image, carousel, collection, Reels)
  - Create Google Ads (search ads with extensions, display banner ads)
  - Prepare assets in all required sizes and formats

- **Creative Optimization Framework**
  - Document which creative elements to test (headline, image, CTA, angle)
  - Define creative rotation strategy
  - Plan how to identify winning creatives early
  - Plan refresh strategy for ad fatigue

**Deliverables**

- **Ad Creative Assets** (all files production-ready)
  - LinkedIn ads (image, carousel, video formats)
  - Facebook/Instagram ads (image, carousel, Reels)
  - Google Ads (text ads, display banner ads)
  - Landing page assets and graphics
  - 10+ ad copy variations
  - Creative brief document

---

### Copywriter

**Activities**

- **Ad Copy Development**
  - Write 10+ variations of ad headlines and body copy
  - Create different angles for different audience segments
  - Develop copy testing framework
  - Write landing page copy (headlines, subheadings, body, CTAs)
  - Write post-conversion email sequences

- **Lead Nurture Copy**
  - Write welcome/confirmation emails
  - Create nurture email sequences (3-5 emails)
  - Develop email subject lines and preview text
  - Write CTA copy that aligns with campaign positioning

**Deliverables**

- **Campaign Copy Library** (5-10 pages)
  - Ad headline and body copy variations (organized by angle)
  - Landing page copy for each variation
  - Form labels and CTA button text
  - Email sequences and subject lines
  - SMS messaging (if mobile messaging is part of campaign)

## Phase 3: Analytics & Conversion Optimization Setup (Week 3)

### CRO Specialist (Conversion Rate Optimization)

**Activities**

- **Baseline Analysis**
  - Review historical conversion rates for similar campaigns
  - Analyze past landing page performance
  - Identify conversion rate benchmarks for industry
  - Document baseline metrics for testing

- **Conversion Tracking Setup**
  - Define conversion events in Google Analytics and ad platforms
  - Set up UTM parameter strategy
  - Document tracking for lead form submissions
  - Plan attribution tracking across touchpoints
  - Set up conversion value tracking (if revenue data available)

- **Heat Map & User Behavior Analysis**
  - Plan heat map and session recording setup
  - Define high-priority pages to analyze
  - Plan user testing or feedback collection
  - Identify usability issues to address

- **Testing Roadmap**
  - Create 12-month testing roadmap
  - Prioritize tests by expected impact
  - Plan pacing for simultaneous tests
  - Define winner declaration criteria

**Deliverables**

- **CRO Strategy & Testing Plan** (5-8 pages)
  - Baseline conversion rates and benchmarks
  - Conversion tracking specifications
  - UTM parameter strategy and implementation
  - 12-month testing roadmap with prioritized tests
  - Winner declaration criteria

---

### Analytics & Pipeline Analyst

**Activities**

- **Analytics Setup & Infrastructure**
  - Configure Google Analytics 4 tracking
  - Set up conversion goals and events
  - Connect CRM data to analytics platform
  - Configure UTM parameter strategy
  - Plan dashboard and reporting structure

- **Attribution & Pipeline Tracking**
  - Define attribution model (first-click, last-click, multi-touch)
  - Map leads to pipeline in CRM
  - Track leads through conversion journey
  - Connect revenue data to marketing activities
  - Plan lead scoring and SQL qualification

- **Reporting & Dashboard Design**
  - Design daily performance dashboard (campaign health)
  - Design weekly reporting dashboard (channel performance)
  - Design monthly dashboard (full funnel analysis)
  - Define KPIs and target metrics
  - Plan automated reporting delivery

**Deliverables**

- **Analytics & Tracking Plan** (5-10 pages)
  - Google Analytics 4 configuration specifications
  - Event tracking plan
  - UTM parameter documentation
  - CRM integration specifications
  - Dashboard mock-ups and KPI definitions
  - Daily/weekly/monthly reporting schedule

## Campaign Performance Metrics & Targets

### Traffic & Engagement Metrics
- **Impressions**: 500K-2M (depending on budget)
- **Clicks**: 5K-20K (target CTR: 1-2%)
- **Click-Through Rate**: 1-2% (industry average 0.5-1%, target above average)

### Lead Generation Metrics
- **Total Leads Generated**: 500-2,000 (depending on budget and targets)
- **Cost Per Lead (CPL)**: $5-20 (target: 20-30% below industry average)
- **Lead Form Conversion Rate**: 10-25% (visitor to lead)
- **Lead Quality**: 30-40% MQL-to-SQL conversion

### Email Metrics
- **Email Open Rate**: 25-35% (industry average 20-30%)
- **Email Click Rate**: 2-4% (industry average 1-3%)
- **Lead nurture effectiveness**: 15-25% of nurture leads become SQLs

### Landing Page Metrics
- **Landing Page Conversion Rate**: 5-15% (industry average 2-5%, target above average)
- **Page Load Time**: <2 seconds
- **Mobile Conversion Rate**: 4-12% (slightly lower than desktop is normal)

### Funnel Metrics
- **Lead to MQL Conversion**: 70-80% (of leads qualify as MQLs)
- **MQL to SQL Conversion**: 30-40% (of MQLs become SQLs)
- **SQL to Customer Conversion**: 10-25% (varies by sales team effectiveness)

### Revenue Metrics
- **Pipeline Created**: $500K-$5M+ (depending on deal size)
- **Cost Per SQL**: $25-100
- **Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)**: 2:1 to 5:1 (depends on deal size)
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: $100-500 (varies by deal size and sales cycle)
- **CAC Payback Period**: 6-12 months
- **Customer Lifetime Value (LTV) to CAC Ratio**: 3:1 or higher

### Campaign Health Metrics
- **Attribution**: 20-35% of new customers influenced by demand gen campaign
- **Time to SQL**: 14-30 days average from first touch to SQL
- **Deal Velocity**: 5-15% faster sales cycles for marketing-influenced deals

## Success Criteria & Go/No-Go Decision Points

### Week 2 Checkpoint
- All campaigns launched successfully
- Tracking verified in all systems
- Initial traffic flowing to landing pages
- Forms capturing leads without issues
- Email automation working correctly

### Week 3 Checkpoint
- Sufficient data to see initial trends (100+ leads)
- CPL approaching target (within 25% of target)
- Conversion rates in reasonable range
- No major technical issues

### Week 4 Checkpoint
- CPL at or near target
- Lead quality meets expectations
- Strong channels identified
- Optimization hypotheses validated

### Week 6 Checkpoint
- Campaign ROI clear (approaching 2:1 ROAS or better)
- Sufficient data for confident recommendations
- Ready for Phase 2 optimization
- Documented learnings for next campaign

## Phase 6: Post-Campaign Analysis (Week 7+)

### All Agents - Campaign Debrief

**Comprehensive Analysis**

#### Campaign Summary
- Total budget spent and ROI achieved
- Total leads generated and cost per lead
- Lead quality and MQL-to-SQL conversion rates
- Pipeline created and revenue influenced
- Channel performance breakdown
- Cost per SQL and CAC by channel

#### What Worked
- Top-performing ad creatives and messaging angles
- Best-performing landing pages and conversion elements
- Highest-quality audience segments
- Most effective email subject lines and sequences

#### What Didn't Work
- Underperforming channels or audiences
- Ad fatigue and creative burnout
- Messaging that didn't resonate
- Audience targeting misses

#### Optimization Opportunities
- Quick wins for Phase 2 campaigns
- Scaling recommendations for best performers
- Areas for continued testing
- Budget allocation improvements

#### Insights for Future Campaigns
- Customer insights validated or revised
- Messaging that resonates documented
- Audience targeting learnings
- Content and creative approaches that work

**Deliverables**

- **Campaign Final Report** (10-15 pages)
  - Executive summary with KPIs
  - Detailed performance by channel
  - Lead quality analysis
  - Comprehensive ROI analysis
  - Top creative and messaging performers
  - Audience performance analysis
  - Optimization recommendations
  - Documented learnings for future campaigns

## Critical Success Factors

1. **Start with Research** - Ground campaign in customer insights, not assumptions
2. **Clear Targeting** - Precisely define and validate target audience
3. **Compelling Messaging** - Use positioning to create persuasive campaigns
4. **Quality Landing Pages** - Invest in conversion optimization from the start
5. **Proper Tracking** - Implement comprehensive tracking and attribution
6. **Continuous Optimization** - Monitor and optimize daily, not weekly
7. **Cross-Functional Alignment** - Regular syncs between all agents
8. **Data-Driven Decisions** - Use analytics to guide optimization
9. **Quality Over Quantity** - Better to do one channel well than many poorly
10. **Patient Optimization** - Allow sufficient time for statistical significance
