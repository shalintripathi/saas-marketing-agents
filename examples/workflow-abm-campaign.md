# Account-Based Marketing (ABM) Campaign Workflow

## Overview

A specialized multi-agent workflow for executing a targeted account-based marketing campaign. This workflow demonstrates how to run highly personalized, multi-channel campaigns against a small number of high-value target accounts (typically 10-100 accounts).

**Timeline**: 3-4 weeks from target list to full execution
**Agents**: 6-8 specialized agents
**Target Accounts**: 10-100 high-value target accounts
**Expected Outcomes**: 3-8 qualified opportunities per target account, $1M-$10M+ in influenced pipeline

---

## Phase 1: Target Account Identification & Research (Weeks 1-2)

### Competitive Intelligence & Account Research

**Activities**

- **Target Account List Development**
  - Define criteria for high-value target accounts
  - Research potential target accounts using intent data and account intelligence tools
  - Analyze company size, revenue, industry, technology stack
  - Evaluate fit against ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
  - Compile initial list of 20-50 target accounts

- **Account-Level Research**
  - Deep dive research on each target account (financials, news, recent initiatives)
  - Identify key decision makers and their roles
  - Research account technology stack and existing competitors
  - Analyze growth initiatives and market position
  - Document recent company changes, funding, hiring

- **Competitive Positioning Analysis**
  - Document how target accounts currently perceive your category
  - Identify which competitors each account is considering
  - Research how competing solutions are positioned to this account
  - Identify account-specific buying criteria
  - Document proof points relevant to each account

**Deliverables**

- **Target Account List** (2-3 pages)
  - 10-100 prioritized target accounts
  - Key firmographics (size, revenue, industry, location)
  - Company description and business model
  - Growth initiatives and business priorities
  - Estimated deal size and likelihood

- **Account Research Profiles** (2-5 pages per account)
  - Company overview and business model
  - Key decision makers (titles, names, responsibilities)
  - Current technology and competitor usage
  - Recent business changes and news
  - Key business initiatives and priorities
  - Relevant proof points and customer references

---

### Customer Insights Researcher

**Activities**

- **Account-Specific Persona Mapping**
  - For each target account, identify key personas involved in decision
  - Research each persona's role, priorities, and success criteria
  - Document what each persona cares about (business impact, technical fit, vendor fit)
  - Identify pain points specific to each persona
  - Map each persona's influence in buying process

- **Account-Specific Pain Point Mapping**
  - Identify top 3-5 pain points for each target account
  - Research specific initiatives or projects driving these pain points
  - Identify quantified business impact for this account type
  - Document how your solution addresses account-specific pain points
  - Identify proof points most relevant to each account

- **Buying Process Documentation**
  - Document typical buying process for accounts of this size
  - Identify typical stakeholders involved
  - Research typical sales cycle length
  - Document key decision criteria for this account type
  - Identify risk factors and common objections

**Deliverables**

- **Account-Specific Persona Maps** (1-2 pages per account)
  - Key personas and their roles
  - Each persona's priorities and success criteria
  - Key concerns and decision criteria for each persona
  - Influence level in buying process
  - Message priorities for each persona

- **Account Buying Process Guide** (2-3 pages)
  - Typical stakeholders for accounts this size
  - Typical buying timeline (days to close)
  - Key decision criteria documented
  - Risk factors and common objections
  - Key trigger events or buying signals

---

## Phase 2: Account-Specific Strategy & Messaging (Week 1-2, Parallel)

### Positioning Strategist & Messaging Architect

**Activities**

- **Account-Specific Positioning**
  - Review account research and identified pain points
  - Develop positioning specifically addressing each account's priorities
  - Create account-specific proof points and evidence
  - Document how positioning specifically applies to each account
  - Develop competitive differentiation for accounts considering alternatives

- **Account-Specific Messaging Development**
  - Create 3-5 message pillars for each account type
  - Develop messaging specific to each account's industry/use case
  - Create messaging addressing identified pain points
  - Develop proof points using relevant customer case studies
  - Create persona-specific messaging variations

- **Sales Conversation Frameworks**
  - Document framework for opening conversations with each account
  - Create conversation starters addressing identified pain points
  - Develop messaging for each key persona
  - Document objection handling by persona and by account type
  - Create talking points for different conversation contexts

**Deliverables**

- **Account-Specific Messaging Framework** (3-5 pages per account type)
  - Problem statement most relevant to this account
  - Positioning specific to account's industry/use case
  - 3-5 core message pillars
  - Persona-specific messaging variations
  - Competitive differentiation messaging
  - Proof points and supporting evidence
  - Objection handling by persona

---

### Outbound Strategist

**Activities**

- **Outreach Strategy by Account**
  - Define entry point and initial contact strategy
  - Determine which persona(s) to target first
  - Develop messaging angle for initial outreach
  - Plan sequence of activities to build urgency
  - Define timeline and pacing of outreach

- **Multi-Channel Engagement Plan**
  - Plan combination of email, LinkedIn, and direct outreach
  - Define email sequence for cold outreach
  - Plan LinkedIn engagement and message strategy
  - Identify outreach/engagement events (webinars, conferences, direct meetings)
  - Plan account-specific offers or incentives to drive engagement

- **Sales Conversation Planning**
  - Develop framework for discovery calls
  - Create conversation guides for different personas
  - Plan follow-up sequences after initial conversation
  - Document success criteria for each conversation
  - Plan how to move conversation forward if initial interest

**Deliverables**

- **Account-Specific Outreach Plan** (2-3 pages per account or account cluster)
  - Entry point strategy
  - Primary contact and backup contacts
  - Initial outreach messaging
  - Multi-channel engagement plan (email, LinkedIn, phone, events)
  - Success criteria and next steps
  - Sales conversation framework

---

## Phase 3: Content & Asset Creation (Weeks 1-2, Parallel)

### Enablement Content Creator

**Activities**

- **Account-Specific Content Development**
  - Create case study featuring similar account or use case
  - Develop one-pager addressing account-specific pain points
  - Create data sheet with account-relevant features
  - Develop competitive comparison for accounts considering alternatives
  - Create risk mitigation documentation (security, compliance, integration)

- **Sales Enablement Materials**
  - Create discovery call guide for account type
  - Develop discovery call slides specific to account
  - Create ROI calculator for account-specific business case
  - Develop competitive battle cards
  - Create objection handling guides by persona

- **Executive Briefing Materials**
  - Develop executive briefing deck (C-level focused)
  - Create industry/use-case specific insights
  - Develop thought leadership pieces for executive engagement
  - Create risk mitigation and success planning documents
  - Develop business value case study

**Deliverables**

- **Account-Specific Content Package** (per account or account type)
  - Case study relevant to account's industry/use case (3-5 pages)
  - One-pager addressing account-specific pain points (1 page)
  - Data sheet highlighting relevant features (2-3 pages)
  - Executive briefing presentation (10-15 slides)
  - Competitive comparison/battle cards
  - ROI calculator or business case template
  - Sales discovery call guide and supporting slides

---

### Content Visual Designer

  - Create one-pager layout and design
  - Design competitive comparison visuals

- **Social & Digital Assets**
  - Design personalized LinkedIn posts or ads for account

  - Design landing page graphics for account-specific campaigns
  - Create webinar or event promotional graphics
  - Design any physical collateral for account meetings

**Deliverables**

- **Account-Specific Visual Assets**
  - One-pager design (print and digital ready)
  - Email graphics and headers
  - Competitive comparison visuals
  - LinkedIn and social graphics
  - Landing page design (if applicable)
  - Executive presentation templates
  - Any printed collateral designs

## Phase 4: Paid Campaign Setup (Weeks 2-3)

### Social Ads Specialist

**Activities**

- **ABM Paid Campaign Strategy**
  - Develop ABM advertising strategy for target accounts
  - Create LinkedIn ABM audience using account and contact lists
  - Plan account-specific ad messaging and creative
  - Develop retargeting strategy for account contacts
  - Plan paid content distribution strategy

- **Campaign Setup**
  - Create audience lists for each target account or account cluster
  - Develop 3-5 ad creative variations by account type
  - Create account-specific landing pages
  - Set up conversion tracking by account
  - Define daily budget and account allocation

- **Creative Development**
  - Design ads specific to account's industry/use case
  - Develop messaging speaking to account-specific pain points
  - Create ads highlighting relevant case studies/proof points
  - Develop ads with account-specific offers or value propositions
  - Create retargeting ads for nurturing relationships

**Deliverables**

- **ABM Paid Campaign Plan** (5-8 pages)
  - Campaign structure and naming convention
  - Target account lists and audience specifications
  - Audience sizes and targeting approach
  - Ad creative variations by account type
  - Landing page strategy
  - Daily budget allocation by account
  - Success metrics and optimization approach

- **Ad Creative Assets**
  - LinkedIn account-based ads (image, video, carousel)
  - Facebook/Instagram ads (if applicable)
  - Retargeting ads for follow-up engagement
  - Account-specific offers or CTAs

## Phase 5: Campaign Launch & Engagement (Week 3)

### All Agents - Coordinated Activation

**Week 1-2 Before Launch**

#### Outbound Strategist & Sales Team
- Load contact lists into email and LinkedIn platforms
- Prepare email sequences and messaging
- Brief sales team on account strategies and messaging
- Schedule outreach activities and calls
- Prepare discovery call guides and talking points

#### Social Ads Specialist
- Launch ABM ad campaigns
- Verify targeting and audience setup
- Monitor initial impressions and engagement
- Set up conversion tracking and measurement

#### Email Coordinator (if separate from Outbound Strategist)
- Verify email deliverability
- Load contact lists and segmentation
- Schedule email sequences
- Monitor opens, clicks, and engagement
- Prepare follow-up sequences

**Week 3 - Campaign Execution**

#### Outbound Strategist & Sales Development
- Execute email outreach sequences
- Engage contacts on LinkedIn (personalized messages, connection requests)
- Monitor initial response rates
- Escalate interested contacts to sales
- Document engagement by account and persona

#### Sales Team
- Execute discovery calls with warm leads
- Use account-specific talking points and discovery guides
- Document conversation outcomes and next steps
- Advance deals through sales process

#### LinkedIn Strategist
- Monitor LinkedIn engagement from contacts
- Respond to messages and engage in conversations
- Continue organic engagement strategy
- Identify engaged contacts for sales follow-up

#### Analytics & Pipeline Analyst
- Track initial engagement metrics
- Monitor email open rates and click rates
- Track ad performance and engagement
- Document which accounts are engaging
- Track early pipeline creation

---

## Phase 6: Monitoring & Optimization (Weeks 3-4+)

### All Agents - Ongoing Optimization

**Weekly Activities**

#### Outbound Strategist
- Analyze outreach response rates by account and by persona
- Identify which messaging angles resonate
- Update sequences based on engagement patterns
- Escalate warm leads to sales immediately
- Plan follow-up sequences for non-responders

#### Sales Team
- Execute sales calls and advance deals
- Use account-specific discovery guides
- Document deal stage and next steps
- Report back on messaging reception
- Provide feedback on prospect objections and concerns

#### LinkedIn Strategist
- Monitor LinkedIn engagement metrics
- Identify most engaged contacts
- Plan targeted connection and message strategies
- Continue organic social engagement
- Track which accounts are most responsive

#### Social Ads Specialist
- Monitor ABM ad performance by account
- Analyze which accounts are engaging with ads
- Adjust targeting if needed
- Refresh creative to prevent fatigue
- Optimize budget allocation to best-performing accounts

#### Analytics & Pipeline Analyst
- Track engagement by account and by persona
- Measure pipeline creation by account
- Calculate ABM-specific metrics (account engagement rate, account conversion rate)
- Identify accounts progressing through sales funnel
- Document which channels drive highest-quality engagement

**Optimization Actions**

- Pause outreach to non-responsive accounts
- Increase engagement with responsive accounts
- Refresh messaging angles that aren't resonating
- Escalate engaged accounts to sales quickly
- Plan account-specific events or meetings for high-priority accounts
- Develop additional content for accounts progressing through sales

## ABM Campaign Metrics & Targets

### Engagement Metrics
- **Account Engagement Rate**: Target 50-70% of target accounts with at least one interaction
- **Contact Engagement Rate**: 20-30% of contacts at target accounts respond/engage
- **Email Open Rate**: 30-40% (higher for ABM personalization)
- **Email Click Rate**: 3-6% (higher than broad campaigns)
- **LinkedIn Engagement Rate**: 5-10% (connections accepted, messages responded to, posts engaged)

### Sales Metrics
- **Conversations Started**: 50-70% of target accounts have at least one sales conversation
- **Qualified Opportunities**: Target 2-4 qualified opportunities per account
- **Opportunity Win Rate**: 15-25% of ABM-generated opportunities convert to customers
- **Average Deal Size**: Higher than general demand gen (typical for target accounts)
- **Sales Cycle Length**: 30-60% shorter than cold outreach (due to warm engagement)

### Pipeline Metrics
- **Pipeline Created per Account**: $250K-$1M+ per target account (depends on deal size)
- **Total Pipeline**: $2.5M-$10M+ (depending on number of accounts and deal size)
- **Pipeline Influenced**: 40-60% of closed deals influenced by ABM campaign
- **Cost Per Qualified Opportunity**: $5K-$25K
- **Cost Per Won Deal**: $50K-$250K+

### Account Progression Metrics
- **Account Progression Rate**: Target 30-50% of target accounts progress to sales stage
- **Account ROI**: Measure ROI per account based on deal size
- **Account Lifetime Value**: Track long-term value of landed accounts

### Campaign Health Metrics
- **Contact List Quality**: 70%+ of contacts are valid/active
- **Contact Engagement**: 30-50% of loaded contacts engage within 30 days
- **Sales Responsiveness**: Sales follows up on warm leads within 24 hours

## Success Metrics & Go/No-Go Decisions

### 2-Week Checkpoint
- Outreach launched to all target accounts
- Initial engagement metrics visible (emails sent, LinkedIn messages sent)
- First response/engagement data coming in
- Ad campaigns live and tracking properly

### 3-Week Checkpoint
- 30-50% of target accounts have had at least one interaction
- First deals moved into sales pipeline
- Early ROI indicators visible
- Message resonance becoming clear

### 4-Week Checkpoint
- Campaign producing qualified opportunities
- Deals advancing through sales funnel
- Clear winners and non-responders identified
- ROI approaching breakeven or positive

### 8-Week Checkpoint
- Multiple opportunities in pipeline per account
- First deals potentially closing
- Account engagement deepening
- Full ROI picture emerging

## Phase 7: Post-Campaign Analysis & Account Nurturing

### Final Campaign Analysis

**Activities**

#### Analytics & Pipeline Analyst
- Calculate final ROI by account
- Analyze which accounts converted to opportunities
- Calculate revenue influenced or won
- Analyze engagement metrics by account type
- Document successful tactics and messaging

#### Outbound Strategist & Sales Team
- Document lessons learned from outreach and sales conversations
- Identify winning messaging angles and talking points
- Document objections and how they were handled
- Plan next phase of engagement for accounts in pipeline
- Identify accounts for long-term nurturing

**Deliverables**

- **ABM Campaign Performance Report** (8-12 pages)
  - Executive summary with ROI and pipeline created
  - Account-by-account performance
  - Engagement metrics by channel
  - Pipeline created and won by account
  - Top performing messaging and offers
  - Lessons learned and recommendations
  - Next phase engagement plan

---

### Long-Term Account Nurturing

For accounts that engaged but didn't convert immediately:
- Continue account-specific nurture sequences
- Plan quarterly business reviews (QBRs) with interested accounts
- Develop multi-year relationship building strategy
- Continue to engage target personas at multiple levels
- Plan next outreach phase with new messaging or value props

## Critical Success Factors

1. **Accurate Account Selection** - Select truly high-value accounts that fit ICP
2. **Thorough Account Research** - Invest time in understanding each account deeply
3. **Account-Specific Personalization** - Don't just use placeholders; truly personalize
4. **Clear Buying Process** - Understand how these accounts make decisions
5. **Sales Alignment** - Sales must be committed to working these accounts
6. **Multi-Channel Approach** - Combine email, LinkedIn, paid, and direct outreach
7. **Quick Sales Escalation** - Move warm leads to sales immediately
8. **Ongoing Optimization** - Monitor engagement weekly and adjust tactics
9. **Long-Term Thinking** - ABM is relationship building, not quick wins
10. **Leadership Engagement** - Engage account decision makers, not just contacts

---

## Common Pitfalls

- Selecting too many accounts (focus on quality over quantity)
- Insufficient account research and personalization
- Not involving sales team early in planning
- Scaling too fast (better to do 10 accounts excellently than 50 poorly)
- Generic messaging that doesn't reflect account research
- Expecting immediate results (ABM takes 60-90 days minimum)
- Not coordinating across channels
- Failing to escalate warm leads to sales
- Not tracking account-level ROI
- Giving up too early on accounts (ABM requires persistence)
