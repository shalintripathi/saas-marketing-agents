# Content Engine Workflow

## Overview

A systematic multi-agent workflow for building and scaling a high-velocity B2B SaaS content production engine. This workflow shows how to produce 8-12 pieces of quality content monthly while maintaining SEO optimization and consistent distribution.

**Timeline**: Ongoing monthly cycles, with 4-week lead time
**Agents**: 6-8 specialized agents
**Production Volume**: 8-12 blog posts, 20+ social assets, 2-4 gated assets per month
**Parallel Tracks**: 3-4 concurrent content pieces in different stages

---

## Overall Framework

### Content Pipeline Stages

1. **Research & Strategy** (Weeks 1-2 of cycle)
   - Keyword research identifies opportunities
   - Blog Strategist plans which topics to tackle
   - Content Optimizer reviews SEO potential

2. **Planning & Outlining** (Week 2-3 of cycle)
   - Blog Strategist creates detailed outlines
   - Content Optimizer ensures SEO alignment
   - Copywriter begins writing

3. **Production & Optimization** (Week 3-4 of cycle)
   - Copywriter produces quality content
   - Content Visual Designer creates graphics
   - Content Optimizer refines for SEO

4. **Review & Distribution** (Week 4-5 of cycle)
   - Content reviewed and finalized
   - Assets prepared for distribution
   - Newsletter Curator schedules distribution
   - LinkedIn Strategist prepares social amplification

5. **Amplification & Monitoring** (Weeks 5+ of cycle)
   - Content distributed across channels
   - Social amplification begins
   - Analytics tracking engagement
   - Content optimized based on performance

## Phase 1: Monthly Planning & Research (Weeks 1-2)

### Keyword Researcher

**Cycle**: Monthly (ongoing)

#### Activities
- **Keyword Opportunity Analysis**
  - Analyze 50-100 keyword opportunities in core business domains
  - Prioritize by search volume, competition, buyer intent, and alignment with positioning
  - Identify keyword clusters and pillar-cluster relationships
  - Map keywords to customer journey stage (awareness, consideration, decision)

- **Content Gap Analysis**
  - Review top-ranking competitors' content for each target keyword
  - Identify gaps where competitors rank for keywords but content is weak
  - Document what makes competitor content rank (word count, structure, comprehensiveness)
  - Identify 15-20 high-potential content opportunities

- **Trend Identification**
  - Monitor industry news, analyst reports, and customer conversations
  - Identify emerging topics gaining search volume
  - Flag trending questions in customer communities and forums
  - Document seasonal keyword trends

- **Historical Performance Review**
  - Analyze which past content ranks well and drives traffic
  - Identify what topics generated highest engagement
  - Calculate content ROI by topic and keyword

#### Deliverables
- **Keyword Opportunity Report** (5-10 pages)
  - 30-50 prioritized keyword opportunities with metrics
  - Search volume, competition score, and business relevance for each
  - Recommended article angle for each keyword
  - Estimated monthly search traffic potential

- **Content Gap Analysis** (3-5 pages)
  - 15-20 high-opportunity content ideas with competitive analysis
  - What competitors are doing and what they're missing
  - How to differentiate
  - Recommended word count and content depth

- **Content Calendar Recommendations**
  - Suggested content topics for next 3 months
  - Estimated search traffic and business value for each
  - Strategic themes and pillar-cluster architecture

---

### Blog Strategist

**Cycle**: Monthly (ongoing)

#### Activities
- **Content Calendar Planning**
  - Review keyword research and trending opportunities
  - Select 8-12 topics to cover this month
  - Plan how content aligns with positioning and messaging
  - Identify pillar content and cluster content relationships
  - Assign publication dates across the month

- **Content Strategy Development**
  - Define which pieces will be core "pillar" content vs. cluster content
  - Plan how pillar and cluster content internally link
  - Identify which topics support specific buyer personas or journey stages
  - Plan mix of educational vs. promotional content (70/30 or 80/20 split)

- **Distribution & Amplification Planning**
  - Plan how each piece will be promoted on social media
  - Plan email inclusion in newsletter or sequences
  - Identify opportunities for paid promotion
  - Plan how content supports sales enablement needs

- **Detailed Outline Development**
  - For each approved topic, develop detailed outline (3-5 pages)
  - Include recommended word count (1,500-2,500 words typical)
  - Include recommended structure and section headings
  - Call out key points to emphasize
  - Identify supporting content or assets needed

#### Deliverables
- **Monthly Content Calendar** (2-3 pages)
  - 8-12 approved topics for the month
  - Tentative publication dates
  - Brief description of each topic
  - Estimated traffic potential
  - Strategic value for each piece

- **Detailed Outlines** (3-5 pages per article, so 24-60 pages total)
  - Article title and slug
  - Target keyword and secondary keywords
  - Recommended word count
  - H2 and H3 structure (full outline)
  - Key talking points and proof points to include
  - Recommended internal links
  - Recommended graphics, data, or supporting assets
  - Persona focus and journey stage

---

### Content Optimizer (SEO)

**Cycle**: Monthly (ongoing) - parallel with Blog Strategist

#### Activities
- **SEO Analysis of Planned Content**
  - For each approved topic from Blog Strategist, conduct SEO review
  - Ensure keyword usage and variations are planned
  - Review recommended word count (is it competitive?)
  - Check SERP features (featured snippets, knowledge panels, etc.)
  - Identify best practices from top-ranking content

- **On-Page SEO Specifications**
  - Define SEO-optimized title tag (50-60 characters)
  - Define SEO-optimized meta description (150-160 characters)
  - Provide keyword usage guidelines for body content
  - Define internal linking strategy for each piece
  - Check for opportunities to answer featured snippet queries

- **Technical SEO Recommendations**
  - Plan structured data markup (schema) for relevant content
  - Identify opportunities for FAQ schema or How-To schema
  - Flag any technical issues with current site SEO
  - Recommend site architecture improvements

- **Link Building Strategy**
  - For each pillar piece, identify 5-10 external link opportunities
  - Identify PR/outreach targets for high-value content
  - Plan how to build links from client case studies, partner sites, etc.

#### Deliverables
- **SEO Specifications Document** (20-30 pages)
  - SEO specs for each of the 8-12 planned articles
  - Title tag, meta description, target keywords
  - Word count benchmarks from top competitors
  - Featured snippet opportunities
  - Internal linking recommendations

- **Link Building Strategy** (3-5 pages)
  - High-value external links to pursue for each pillar piece
  - Outreach targets and contact information
  - Link anchor text recommendations
  - Competitive link analysis

## Phase 2: Content Production (Weeks 3-4)

### Copywriter

**Cycle**: Ongoing (8-12 articles monthly)

#### Activities per Article
- **Research & Outlining** (1-2 hours)
  - Review keyword research, blog outline, and SEO specs
  - Conduct additional research if needed
  - Prepare research materials and sources
  - Outline key structure

- **First Draft** (3-4 hours)
  - Write comprehensive first draft
  - Integrate keywords naturally throughout
  - Include supporting data, statistics, and quotes
  - Add relevant examples and case studies
  - Target recommended word count (1,500-2,500 words)

- **Revision & Optimization** (1-2 hours)
  - Optimize for readability and scannability
  - Add subheadings, bullet points, numbered lists where appropriate
  - Integrate CTAs and conversions goals
  - Ensure brand voice and tone consistency
  - Check for grammar, spelling, accuracy

- **Messaging Alignment Check**
  - Ensure article aligns with positioning and messaging
  - Check that key proof points are included
  - Verify competitive narrative alignment

#### Monthly Production Target
- **8-12 blog articles** (1,500-2,500 words each)
- **2-4 pillar pieces** (2,500-4,000 words, comprehensive)
- **6-8 cluster pieces** (1,500-2,000 words, focused)
- **4-6 email newsletter pieces** (500-750 words)
- **Supporting copy** (landing pages, ads, social posts)

#### Deliverables
- **Blog Articles** (8-12)
  - Complete draft articles in Google Docs or Markdown
  - Formatted with proper headings
  - SEO specifications applied
  - Internal linking recommendations called out
  - Asset requirements listed (graphics needed, etc.)

---

### Content Visual Designer

**Cycle**: Ongoing (parallel with copywriting)

#### Activities
- **Article Graphics** (per published article)
  - Feature image for article top (1,200x600 or 1,600x840)
  - Internal graphics (infographics, data visualizations, diagrams)
  - Social media graphics for article promotion (3-5 variations)
  - Email thumbnail graphics for newsletter inclusion

- **Asset Creation**
  - Create infographics visualizing key data points
  - Design data visualizations for statistics mentioned in content
  - Create process diagrams or workflow visuals
  - Design supporting worksheets or templates

- **Brand Consistency**
  - Apply brand colors, fonts, and style guidelines
  - Ensure visual consistency across multiple pieces
  - Create template assets for reuse across pieces

- **Social Asset Variations**
  - Create 3-5 variations of social graphics per article
  - Different formats for LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook
  - Different copy and visual emphasis for testing

#### Monthly Production Target
- **8-12 feature images** (for blog articles)
- **20-30 internal graphics** (infographics, diagrams, charts)
- **40-60 social media graphics** (multiple formats and variations)
- **4-8 gated asset designs** (checklists, templates, worksheets)

#### Deliverables
- **Blog Graphics**
  - Feature images (1 per article, ready to publish)
  - Internal graphics (2-3 per article)

- **Social Media Graphics**
  - 3-5 variations per article
  - Formatted for LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook
  - High-resolution and mobile versions

- **Supporting Assets**
  - Infographics and data visualizations
  - Gated asset designs
  - Email graphics and thumbnails

## Phase 3: Optimization & Final Review (Week 4)

### Content Optimizer (SEO)

#### Activities
- **On-Page SEO Verification**
  - Verify keyword usage throughout article
  - Check for keyword overuse or awkward integration
  - Ensure natural language and readability
  - Check internal linking is in place
  - Verify title tag, meta description are optimized

- **Content Quality Check**
  - Verify accuracy of claims and statistics
  - Check for proper citations and sources
  - Ensure competitive analysis is current
  - Verify examples are relevant and helpful

- **Technical Readiness Check**
  - Check proper heading hierarchy (H1, H2, H3)
  - Verify image alt text is descriptive
  - Ensure proper URL structure and slug
  - Check for structured data implementation

#### Deliverables
- **Final SEO Checklist** (per article)
  - Verification that all SEO specs are met
  - Any remaining optimizations needed
  - Sign-off for publication readiness

---

### Blog Strategist (Final Review)

#### Activities
- **Editorial Review**
  - Verify article meets publishing standards
  - Check alignment with content calendar
  - Verify internal linking strategy is implemented
  - Approve for publication

#### Deliverables
- **Publication Approval** (per article)
  - Sign-off on final article
  - Assignment of publication date and time
  - Final distribution instructions

---

## Phase 4: Distribution & Amplification (Week 4-5)

### Newsletter Curator

**Cycle**: Weekly/Bi-weekly (ongoing)

#### Activities
- **Newsletter Planning**
  - Decide which recent blog content to feature
  - Plan newsletter layout and flow
  - Coordinate with other email sends to avoid frequency issues
  - Plan seasonal themes and special edition newsletters

- **Content Curation**
  - For each newsletter: select 3-5 recent blog posts
  - Select 2-3 external curated content pieces
  - Select 1-2 case studies or customer stories
  - Plan which content gets featured vs. secondary placement

- **Newsletter Preparation**
  - Coordinate with copywriter on newsletter article summaries
  - Coordinate with designer on newsletter template and graphics
  - Set up newsletter segments based on content topic interests
  - Plan newsletter send time and frequency

- **Distribution**
  - Manage newsletter send scheduling
  - Monitor open rates and click-through rates
  - Test subject lines and sending times
  - Update segments based on engagement

#### Deliverables
- **Monthly Newsletter Calendar** (1-2 pages)
  - Newsletter send schedule
  - Planned content features for each issue
  - Segment strategy

- **Newsletter Templates & Copy**
  - Newsletter template design (or update existing)
  - Newsletter copy and summary text for content

---

### LinkedIn Strategist

**Cycle**: Ongoing (daily social amplification)

#### Activities
- **Content Amplification Planning**
  - For each published blog post, plan LinkedIn distribution strategy
  - Create 3-5 LinkedIn post variations
  - Plan multi-week amplification (not just launch day)
  - Plan carousel posts, document posts, video content

- **Organic Social Execution**
  - Post LinkedIn articles or document posts with article excerpts
  - Create conversation-starting posts highlighting key content insights
  - Share employee advocacy posts
  - Engage with audience comments and questions
  - Tag relevant people and companies

- **Community Engagement**
  - Identify LinkedIn groups and communities relevant to content
  - Share content to appropriate groups
  - Engage in discussions mentioning related topics
  - Build relationships with relevant LinkedIn influencers

- **LinkedIn Analytics & Optimization**
  - Track which content resonates most on LinkedIn
  - Analyze post performance metrics
  - Test different posting times and formats
  - Identify top-performing content for paid amplification

#### Deliverables
- **LinkedIn Amplification Plan** (per article)
  - 3-5 LinkedIn post copy variations
  - Publishing schedule (spread over 2-4 weeks)
  - Hashtag recommendations
  - Engagement strategy

- **Weekly LinkedIn Report**
  - Top-performing posts
  - Engagement metrics
  - Follower growth and audience insights
  - Optimization recommendations

---

## Phase 5: Monitoring & Optimization (Weeks 5+)

### Analytics & Performance Tracking

**Cycle**: Continuous (ongoing)

#### Activities
- **Content Performance Tracking**
  - Monitor traffic to each published piece
  - Track keyword rankings for target keywords
  - Monitor engagement metrics (time on page, scroll depth, etc.)
  - Track conversion rates and leads generated per content piece

- **Traffic & SEO Analysis**
  - Identify which content drives most organic traffic
  - Track keyword ranking improvements over time
  - Monitor competitive rankings for target keywords
  - Identify content needing optimization for improved rankings

- **Lead & Pipeline Impact**
  - Track how many leads came from each content piece
  - Analyze lead quality from different content sources
  - Track content influence on pipeline and deals
  - Calculate content ROI by piece and by topic

- **User Behavior Analysis**
  - Analyze scroll depth and engagement on articles
  - Track internal link click-through rates
  - Identify content that causes user drop-off
  - Determine which sections get most attention

- **Competitive Benchmarking**
  - Track how your content ranks vs. competitors
  - Monitor competitor content performance
  - Identify content ranking opportunities
  - Track competitive content creation and strategy changes

#### Deliverables
- **Weekly Content Performance Report** (2-3 pages)
  - Traffic by piece (last 7 days)
  - Leads generated by source
  - Top-performing content
  - Optimization opportunities

- **Monthly Content Analytics Report** (5-10 pages)
  - Content traffic by topic and type
  - Keyword ranking progress
  - Lead generation by content source
  - Content ROI analysis
  - Competitive positioning
  - Optimization recommendations for next month

- **Content Optimization Recommendations**
  - Articles to refresh or optimize
  - Link building opportunities
  - Content gap opportunities
  - Emerging keyword trends to address

## Critical Success Factors

1. **Consistent Publishing Schedule** - Publish on a predictable schedule (e.g., 2-3x weekly)
2. **Content Quality Over Quantity** - Better to publish 4 great pieces than 12 mediocre ones
3. **Keyword Research Foundation** - Every piece should start with keyword research
4. **SEO Integration from the Start** - Don't optimize for SEO after writing; build it in
5. **Data & Examples** - Use real data, stats, and examples to build credibility
6. **Internal Linking Strategy** - Deliberately link between pillar and cluster content
7. **Consistent Distribution** - Amplify content across multiple channels and over time
8. **Regular Audits** - Review top-performing content and optimize underperformers
9. **Team Specialization** - Each agent focuses on their core expertise
10. **Clear Handoffs** - Each phase has clear inputs and outputs with approval gates

---

## Common Pitfalls

- Writing first, researching keywords second
- Producing content in isolation without SEO optimization
- Expecting immediate results (organic ranking takes 6-12 months)
- Underestimating design and graphics time
- Neglecting internal linking strategy
- Not amplifying content across social channels
- Creating content without clear conversion goals
- Stopping optimization after publication
- Not tracking content performance and ROI
- Publishing inconsistently
